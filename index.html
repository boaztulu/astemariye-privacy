Privacy Policy — አስተማሪዬ (Astemariye)

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application አስተማሪዬ (package com.boaztulu.astemariye, the “App”), published by Boaz Tulu, handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information the App itself collects

The App is an offline learning app. It does not require you to create an account, and it does not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, location, or files. Your learning progress, favorites, and settings are stored only on your device.

2. Feedback you choose to send

If you use the “Suggest a correction” feature, the App sends the word identifier, the language, and your optional note to our database so we can improve the translations and pronunciations. This feedback does not include your name or any personal identifier.

3. Advertising

The App is free and supported by ads provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information, including device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), general location inferred from IP address, and ad-interaction data. This is governed by Google’s policies:

  • How Google uses information from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device’s Settings → Google → Ads.

4. Children

The App contains educational content suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data retention & security

On-device data remains until you uninstall the App or clear its data. Feedback records are kept only to improve the App’s content.
